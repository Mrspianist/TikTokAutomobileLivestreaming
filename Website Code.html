    Our Service

    The TikTok platform provides short video marketing for automobile companies. Automobile companies could open their own accounts to send short videos to attract customers and add car information and customer appointment test drive connections in the video to efficiently and accurately attract more customers.

    The TikTok platform can also provide automobile star map products. This service invites celebrities on TikTok to promote cars in short videos. Automobile companies could cooperate with internet celebrities with large amount of fans on the TikTok platform, like implanting video advertisements and car information links in advertisement short videos. This service could efficiently attract customers for automobile companies.

    The TikTok platform provides online live streaming sales services for automobile dealers. Many sales in dealer stores have joined the live streaming methods. In the epidemic environment, communication with customers can be completed online. TikTok platform allows automobile stores to reach more customers. 

    Marketing Plan

    TikTok live streaming automobile department provide creative marketing plan solutions. These marketing plans provide car cloud store service for automobile companies, which has distribution of customer information in different places. 

    By distributing different customer location to the appropriate dealers, 80% of the different location customer clues changed from invalid to effective.
